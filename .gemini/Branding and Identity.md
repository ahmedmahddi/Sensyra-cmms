# 04 - Branding and Identity

## Project Name

**Sensyra**

### Name Rationale

The name "Sensyra" is designed to convey intelligence, awareness, and system-wide monitoring without being tied specifically to hardware or physical sensors. It evokes a sense of proactive control.

---

## Brand Inspiration

- **Animal:** Spider
- **Concept:** The spider represents excellence in monitoring, early detection, and systems thinking. Its web is a network that senses the smallest vibrations, allowing for a swift, coordinated responseâ€”a perfect metaphor for a modern CMMS.

## Visual Style

- **Geometry:** Abstract web or network-like geometric patterns.
- **Colors:** Deep blues and graphite tones to convey professionalism, intelligence, and stability.
- **Design Philosophy:** Modern, clean, and minimalistic.
